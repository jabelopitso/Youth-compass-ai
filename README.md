Youth Compass AI is more than just a job board; it's a long-term human capital development system that uses AI to create a self-improving, scalable, and localized platform that reduces the cost of entry to the economy for millions of young South African youth.

## üöÄ Core AI Features

### 1. Contextual Profiler (The Brain)
- **NLP Personality & Skills Mapping**: Analyzes natural language responses to create transferable skills profiles
- **Reinforcement Learning Pathway Engine**: Continuously learns which pathways lead to successful employment
- **Overcomes Barriers**: No CV required, works with low literacy levels

### 2. Economic & Educational Choices
- **AI-Driven Course Relevance Scoring**: Ranks courses by Cost, Proximity, and Job Market Value
- **Hidden Job Market Scanner**: NLP model that finds opportunities beyond traditional job boards
- **ROI Optimization**: Prevents mis-spending on irrelevant qualifications

### 3. Sustainable Support System
- **Ethical Conversational AI**: 24/7 culturally-aware motivational coaching
- **Interest-Based Peer Matching**: Connects youth with similar career paths and challenges
- **Mental Health Support**: Addresses resilience and motivation barriers
